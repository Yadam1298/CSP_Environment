About the Project

Community Service Project — Environmental Protection

Project Summary

This community service project surveyed 50 local residents to assess environmental awareness, pollution issues, and the application of the 3R principles in daily life. The survey covered Velvadam, Vijayawada, and Muthyalampadu.

Objectives

  • Assess public awareness about environmental issues.
  • Compare urban and rural perspectives on environmental protection.
  • Encourage adoption of the Reduce, Reuse, Recycle principles.
